About me

RASA DRUKTEINYTE

Rasa Drukteinyte is a lithuanian artist, who was born in Klaipeda.

Rasa has a background in a variety of artistic mediums and education. In 2010 she obtained a BA in Visual Arts from Art Academy in Klaipeda (Lithuania). In 2013 - 2015 Rasa studied in Sustainable Design & Business (AP) at Copenhagen School of Design & Technology and in 2021 Rasa graduated in Communication & Social Psychology (BA) from Roskilde University.

Rasa possesses a personal and professional interest in art but also in global cultures, human relationships.Therefore, Rasa's works take a look behind inherited structures in society, questioning human behaviours. Her use of edgy irony and sarcasm in her works is her artist signature.
